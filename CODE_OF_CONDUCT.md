# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Nova to be a friendly, welcoming project for everyone, regardless of
experience level, background, or identity. We pledge to make participation a
harassment-free experience for all.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that helps the community:

- Being kind, respectful, and patient — especially with beginners.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what's best for the project and its users.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory jokes and language.
- Personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these
standards. Reports of unacceptable behavior can be made privately through
GitHub (for example, using the repository's **Security → Report a vulnerability**
channel for sensitive reports, or by contacting a maintainer). All reports will
be reviewed and handled with discretion.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and comments that don't
follow this Code of Conduct.
